DOG DAYS

JON SMITH

WHEN ALL IS LOST, ALL WE ARE ARE ANIMALS. Against a desolate landscape tattered by catastrophic war, 13-year-old Lisa befriends a man who has chosen to live as a dog, begging for scraps of food at a time when scraps is all anyone has. As seasons pass, rations diminish. For Lisa and her family, it seems all they have left is their humanity, but how long before that is also lost? Based on the story Dog Days By Judith Budnitz.
